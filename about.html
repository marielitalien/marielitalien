Marie-Andrée L'Italien

After assisting over 250 small and medium businesses all over the province of Québec in the Web industry, Marie-Andrée has an excellent overview of the major desires and needs of a large clientele.

Whether it is in the clothing industry, food industry and more, she is adaptable in learning, listening and providing key points in how to make a small business work online. By giving SEO tips, photography tricks, proofing, paragraph displays, social media marketing advice and even more - now many businesses thrive in their work with their online store in Québec.

Marie-Andrée graduated from Concordia University in Montréal in 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Design.

She is also a passionate illustrator, portraitist and animator.
